  • Gear Value Analysis: Track total purchase value and insured values across multiple currencies
  • Lens Coverage Charts: Visualize your focal length and aperture coverage with interactive charts and gap analysis
How much does GearGrove Pro cost?

GearGrove Pro is a one-time purchase with lifetime access. No subscription, no recurring fees. The price varies by region and is displayed in your local currency in the App Store.

Can I try Pro features before buying?

When you tap on a Pro feature (Coverage Analysis, Gear Value, Warranty Tracking, or CSV Export/Import), you'll see a detailed explanation of what it does before purchasing.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open GearGrove on your new device, tap any Pro feature, then tap "Restore Purchase" on the paywall screen. Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a lens to my collection?

Tap the "+" button in the main view. You can either browse the database of 195+ pre-loaded lenses (tap "Browse Common Lenses"), or manually enter all lens details. When browsing, filter by brand and mount to quickly find your lens.

How do I browse the lens database?

When adding a lens, tap "Browse Common Lenses". Use the brand and mount filters at the top to narrow down results. The database includes 195+ lenses from Canon, Nikon, Sony, Sigma, Tamron, and other major brands. Tap any lens to auto-fill its specifications.

How do I edit a lens?

Tap on any lens in your collection to view its details, then tap the edit button (pencil icon) in the top right. You can modify any information including specifications, purchase details, ratings, and notes.

How do I delete a lens?

In the main list view, swipe left on any lens and tap "Delete". You'll be asked to confirm the deletion. Alternatively, you can mark a lens as "Sold" to keep its records while removing it from active inventory.

How do I rate my lenses?

When adding or editing a lens, scroll to the "Purpose & Rating" section. Tap the stars to rate from 1-5, or tap a filled star to remove the rating. You can also sort your collection by rating to see your favorites first.

How does the coverage analysis work? PRO

Tap the chart icon in the toolbar to view your collection's coverage (requires Pro). The analysis shows: 1) Focal length coverage across your collection, 2) Aperture range visualization, 3) Focal length vs aperture scatter plot, and 4) Gap analysis identifying missing focal lengths or aperture ranges.

How do I track warranties? PRO

With GearGrove Pro, when adding or editing a lens, scroll to "Insurance (Optional)" and tap "Add Warranty Expiry Date". Enter the warranty end date and optionally add warranty details. Lenses expiring within 30 days will trigger alerts on the main screen.

How do I view my collection's total value? PRO

With GearGrove Pro, tap the dollar sign icon ($) in the toolbar to view the Gear Value screen. This shows total purchase value and insured value by currency, plus a sortable list of all lenses from most to least expensive.

How do I export my collection? PRO

With GearGrove Pro, go to the Import/Export screen (tap the up arrow icon). Tap "Export to CSV" to create a backup file of your entire collection including all specifications, ratings, purchase info, warranty data, and notes.

How do I import lenses from CSV? PRO

With GearGrove Pro, go to Import/Export and tap "Import from CSV". Select your CSV file. The importer automatically detects duplicates and skips them. After import, you'll see a summary of total records, successfully imported, and skipped duplicates.

How do I filter and sort my collection?

Use the filter bar at the bottom of the main screen (iPhone) or left sidebar (iPad). You can: 1) Sort by brand, rating, focal length, aperture, weight, purchase date, or release year, 2) Filter by lens type (Prime/Zoom), brand, mount, 3) Show only weather-sealed or stabilized lenses, 4) Include/exclude sold lenses.

Is my data stored in the cloud?

No. All your data is stored locally on your device. GearGrove doesn't sync to any cloud service, which means your lens collection data stays completely private and secure on your device. You control your data through manual CSV exports (Pro feature).

Tips & Best Practices

  • Use the Browse database feature to quickly add lenses with accurate specifications
  • Add serial numbers to help with insurance claims and theft recovery
  • Rate your lenses to identify favorites and underused gear
  • Consider upgrading to Pro for warranty tracking and collection insights
  • Mark lenses as sold rather than deleting to maintain history

Overview

GearGrove is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our photography lens inventory management application.

Data Collection and Storage

Local Storage Only: GearGrove stores all your data locally on your device. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on our servers or third-party servers.

The following information is stored locally on your device:

  • Lens specifications (brand, model, focal length, aperture, etc.)
  • Purchase information (date, price, currency, condition)
  • Personal ratings and notes
  • Usage tracking data
  • Insurance and warranty information (Pro feature)
  • Serial numbers and firmware versions
  • Any other data you enter into the app

In-App Purchases

GearGrove offers an optional one-time purchase (GearGrove Pro) processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system. We do not collect or store any payment information. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple. Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device signed in with the same Apple ID.

No Account Required

GearGrove does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or log in with any service. Your data remains completely private and under your control.

No Internet Connection Required

GearGrove works completely offline and does not require an internet connection to function. The app does not communicate with any external servers or services, except when processing In-App Purchases through Apple's secure payment system.

Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or trade any of your information with third parties. Since all data is stored locally on your device, we have no access to your lens collection or personal information.

Data Export

You have complete control over your data. With GearGrove Pro, you can export your lens collection in CSV format at any time. When you export data, you control where the file is saved and who has access to it.

Analytics and Tracking

GearGrove does NOT use any analytics services, tracking tools, or advertising networks. We do not collect usage statistics, crash reports, or any other information about how you use the app.

Third-Party Services

GearGrove does not integrate with or use any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that could access your data, with the exception of Apple's In-App Purchase system for processing Pro feature purchases.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, etc.). We recommend keeping your device secure and regularly backing up your data using the CSV export feature (Pro).

Children's Privacy

GearGrove does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we don't collect any data, the app can be safely used by users of all ages.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the app from your device. Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all your lens collection data. Your In-App Purchase history remains tied to your Apple ID and can be restored if you reinstall the app.
