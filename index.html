Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 7, 2025

Overview

GearGrove is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our photography lens inventory management application.

Data Collection and Storage

Local Storage Only: GearGrove stores all your data locally on your device. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on our servers or third-party servers.

The following information is stored locally on your device:

  • Lens specifications (brand, model, focal length, aperture, etc.)
  • Purchase information (date, price, currency, condition)
  • Personal ratings and notes
  • Usage tracking data
  • Insurance and warranty information
  • Serial numbers and firmware versions
  • Any other data you enter into the app

No Account Required

GearGrove does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or log in with any service. Your data remains completely private and under your control.

No Internet Connection Required

GearGrove works completely offline and does not require an internet connection to function. The app does not communicate with any external servers or services.

Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or trade any of your information with third parties. Since all data is stored locally on your device, we have no access to your lens collection or personal information.

Data Export

You have complete control over your data. You can export your lens collection in CSV format at any time. When you export data, you control where the file is saved and who has access to it.

Analytics and Tracking

GearGrove does NOT use any analytics services, tracking tools, or advertising networks. We do not collect usage statistics, crash reports, or any other information about how you use the app.

Third-Party Services

GearGrove does not integrate with or use any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that could access your data.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, etc.). We recommend keeping your device secure and regularly backing up your data using the CSV export feature.

Children's Privacy

GearGrove does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we don't collect any data, the app can be safely used by users of all ages.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the app from your device. Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all your lens collection data.
